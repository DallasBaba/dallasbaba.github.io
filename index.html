Environmental Emissions Dashboard - EPA.gov (Dec., 2025)

↗
CHALLENGE

Agencies and utilities often struggle to interpret large, complex environmental datasets in a way that drives decision‑making. The challenge is to transform eGRID2022 emissions data into an intuitive, actionable monitoring tool for both technical and non‑technical users.

SOLUTION

The report integrates KPI summaries, plant‑level insights, generator details, and visual breakdowns of emissions and generation output. It streamlines environmental monitoring and supports AI evaluation workflows for emissions management.

OUTCOME

The dashboard enables regulators, analysts, and facility operators to identify emission patterns, compliance risks, and operational inefficiencies. Users gain a clearer understanding of NOx performance across regions and facility types, improving transparency and data‑driven decision‑making.

Power BI Data Factory SQL Server DAX

Social Capital Analytics (June, 2024)

↗
CHALLENGE

The challenge is to transform the massive Meta Social Capital dataset, built from 21 billion friendship connections into insights that reveal where economic connectedness is strong or weak. Policymakers need clear, data‑driven guidance to understand these disparities and address them effectively.

SOLUTION

Our team built an end‑to‑end analytics framework using Microsoft Fabric, supported by Tableau, SAS Viya, and Generative AI, to process and model the Social Capital dataset at scale. Through machine learning and statistical analysis, we identified patterns in economic connectedness across states, counties, and educational environments.

OUTCOME

The project equip decision‑makers with precise, evidence‑based insights that highlight social capital gaps and opportunities for intervention. The solution lays the foundation for more resilient, connected, and opportunity‑rich local ecosystems.
